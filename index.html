About Me

This year, I decided to say yes to new challenges and to the future I want to build. I’m a Software Engineer in training focused on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, DOM manipulation, responsive design, accessibility, and Git/GitHub.

I serve as the Vice President of Student Government at Calbright College, where I support student initiatives and help create a community rooted in empathy and collaboration. Leadership has shaped the way I approach tech.

I’m Brazilian, a lifelong learner, and a creative who loves watercolor and exploring the intersection between art and technology.

Technology is where imagination becomes something real. As Eminem says: success is my only option, failure is not.
